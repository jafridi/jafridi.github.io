Some of the projects I'm proud to have worked on:

MortgageHippo

MortgageHippo is the first fully automated tool designed to guide homebuyers through every step of the mortgage process to buy their dream home. We built a solution that is both fun and functional, all with the goal of creating a better user experience to get a mortgage.


My role included design and front-end development of the closing plan dashboard and marketing pages, including the homepage.


Yezzi

Yezzi was a peer-to-peer money transfer platform to help the underbanked, or those without access to traditional financial services, send money to their loved ones abroad.


My role included designing end-to-end user and product flows as well as design of marketing pages, including the homepage.
